SublimeLinter 3.4.29
---------------------

To lower the amount of administration we have to do, the google group has been
deprecated. From now on, please follow these steps when seeking support or
completing a bug report:

- Read the installation and troubleshooting documentation at http://sublimelinter.readthedocs.org/

- Search the now deprecated SublimeLinter Google Group at https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/sublimelinter

- Search the issue tracker at https://github.com/SublimeLinter/SublimeLinter3/issues

if you can't find an issue or post for your problem, please submit one in the issue tracker.
Be sure to put your OS, SublimeLinter version, and **ENTIRE** debug console output in the issue.


NOW GO CREATE SOMETHING AWESOME!!
